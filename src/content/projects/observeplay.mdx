ObservePlay investigates how observing real user interaction can drive accessibility personalization, especially in interactive and game contexts where needs vary widely.

## Concept

Rather than relying solely on static preferences, ObservePlay derives accessibility profiles from observed behavior, allowing experiences to adapt to how a person actually interacts.

## Approach

The project captures interaction signals during play and use, then translates those observations into actionable accessibility profiles and accommodations.

## Why It Matters

Observation-based profiling can surface accessibility needs that users may not explicitly configure, helping create experiences that adapt intelligently and inclusively.
